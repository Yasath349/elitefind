Three steps, no sign-up

How It Works

Elite Find is intentionally simple: one person shares, one person tracks, both meet in the middle.

01

Open & Share

Open Elite Find and hit Start Sharing. A unique Session ID and QR code are generated instantly — nothing to type, nothing to sign up for.

02

Enter & Connect

Your friend opens Track, enters the 6-character code (or scans the QR), and connects to your live position in seconds.

03

Meet in the Middle

Both of you watch the satellite map update in real time, with a routed path and distance — until a chime tells you you've arrived.

Why we built this

About Elite Find

Crowded places are wonderful right up until you lose the person you came with. Elite Find exists to close that gap — for the length of one meetup, and not a second longer.

Session-based, not account-based

There's no profile to build and no contact list to maintain. A session exists only as long as you're actively sharing.

Consent on both ends

Sharing starts only when you tap Start Sharing, and tracking works only for someone who has the exact Session ID you gave them.

Open, inspectable tech

Built with Leaflet, OSRM and Firebase — no proprietary black box, so you always know what's running under the hood.
